Speak to a Robot

Hal 9000 is a sentient computer that controls the Discovery One spaceship. You must ask Hal to open the spaceship's pod bay doors when needed. The spaceship has many pod bay doors, enter a number and Hal will respond with the same number if he can open the door or say sorry if he can not. From time to time Hal has a malfunction and replies with a "Boop!" or "Beep!". Due to system requirements, Hal will attempt to open every pod bay door up to the number you provide.
